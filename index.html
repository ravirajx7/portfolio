About Me

I'm Ravi Raj(रवि राज), a human being who works as an Engineer. I am originally from Ranchi, Jharkhand, India. I was born on september 8, 1996. I speak and write Hindi and English.


I enjoy exploring new places and websites. Discussing philosophy, cricket, and music are some of the favourite parts which I enjoy as human. I'm a free thinker who believes everyone should live their life in the way they like and should enjoy it to the fullest while it lasts.


I was privileged to get my first computer when I was 9 years old after fighting with my parents for playing games like GTA Vice City and IGI. Apart from games I liked drawing flags and houses in MS Paint


The real passion for engineering got sparked in me by my very first experience with programming in Logo when it was taught in my school.


It really struck me when I got to know that things in computers are not magic but actually set of instructions given to it. It motivated me to understand the technology in depth thus I decided to pursue my career in engineering.


I thought just having motivation was enough but when I started struggling in writing simple programmes like Binary Search and Bubble Sort, I started questioned by ability. It was only when I broke the problems into smaller sub-problems and started being more curious I learnt the art of programming.


Since then, I learnt and explored many different technologies. I enjoyed breaking things to get better understanding. I enjoyed reading news at Hacker News and Slashdot which are truly the things which has always inspired me to continue doing this.


I got my first job right after my college and got very good guidance from my colleague and manager. I helped my team in building payment microservices and in e-commerce web application development. It was this company which gave me exposure for talking to clients and led me to learn the value of contextual knowledge in business. I also got to know that coding is the easiest part of developer's job. I joined Rocket Software and helped my team in design, development and maintenance of a content management system.


Although, I have done full stack development since 2015, I enjoy doing backend development the most. I have experience of provisioning and managing infrastructure for enterprise applications. I am most proficient in Java, Spring and cloud native technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
